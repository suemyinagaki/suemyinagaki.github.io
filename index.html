About Me

I am a Research Assistant at Visualization and Data Analytics Research Center from New York University's Tandon School of Engineering.

Currently I am a first-year PhD student at NYU under the supervision of Prof. Claudio Silva. My research interests are (but not limited to) computer vision, deep learning, human computer interaction and computer graphics. I want computers to have the same visual understanding of the world as we humans normally do.
